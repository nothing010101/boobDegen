Understanding Futures Trading

Master the fundamentals of cryptocurrency futures trading

What Are Crypto Futures?

Cryptocurrency futures are financial contracts that allow traders to buy or sell digital assets at a predetermined price on a specific future date. Unlike spot trading, futures enable you to profit from both rising and falling markets through long and short positions.

Long Positions

Betting that the price will go up. You profit when the asset price increases above your entry point.

Short Positions

Betting that the price will go down. You profit when the asset price decreases below your entry point.

Leverage Trading

Use borrowed capital to increase your position size and potential profits (but also risks).

Risk Management

Set stop-loss and take-profit orders to automatically manage your risk and secure profits.
